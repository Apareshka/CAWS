According to UN statistics, each year about 90 000 people die due to natural hazards and more than 160 bilion get affected by them. Sure, some people are more vulnerable to this disasters because of geographical and economical factors. Our mission is to find those who have the biggest risk.

How does it work?

We collect and analize information for natural disasters and economics and demographics for each country, calculating it's Natural Hazard Risk Coeficient (NHRC). Larger coeficient means larger threat for people, so we can define which social group is the most vulnerable.
